Privacy Policy - Be Still

Last updated: 2026-06-29

Be Still is designed to collect as little as possible. In practice, that is nothing.

What we collect

Nothing. Be Still does not collect, transmit, or store any of your data on any server. We have no servers, no analytics, and no accounts.

Where your data lives

Your sittings (date, duration, stillness score, movement count) are stored only on your device. If you pair an Apple Watch, completed watch sittings are sent to your own iPhone using Apple's WatchConnectivity, device to device. They are never sent to us.

Apple Health (optional)

If you turn on heart rate or "log mindful minutes" in Settings, Be Still reads heart rate during a sit and/or writes a Mind & Body session to your own Apple Health store. This is controlled by you, stays within Apple Health, and is never read or collected by us.

Motion sensor

Be Still reads your device's motion sensor only while a sitting is active, to measure stillness. Readings are processed on device in real time and are not recorded raw or transmitted.

Children

Be Still does not collect data from anyone, including children.
